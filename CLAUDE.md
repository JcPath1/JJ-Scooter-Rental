## Project Overview

# J & J Scooter Rentals - Shopify Website

## Overview
Shopify website for scooter and wagon rentals serving Canton First Monday Trade Days ("World's Largest Flea Market") in Canton, Texas. Integrated booking/scheduling system with SMS notifications and admin dashboard.

## Business Info
- **Business:** J & J Scooter Rentals
- **Phone:** 972-979-1722
- **Address:** 367 North Trade Days Blvd., Canton, TX 75103
- **Location:** 1 mile south of I-20 on Hwy 19, next to IAMERICAFLAGS

## Brand Colors
- **Primary:** Bright Red (#FF0000)
- **Secondary:** Yellow (#FFD700)
- **Tagline:** "Why Walk When You Can Ride?"
- **Marketing Hook:** World's Largest Trade Days / Flea Market

## Hours (Trade Days Only)
- Thursday-Saturday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Sunday: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

## Inventory (100 Units)
- Standard Scooters (4-wheel): 55 units - 350 lb capacity
- Big & Tall Scooters (4-wheel HD): 20 units - 500 lb capacity
- Pull Wagons: 25 units

## Tech Stack
- Platform: Shopify
- Booking App: BookThatApp (or Sesami)
- SMS: Built into booking app or SMSBump/Postscript
- Calendar: Only Trade Days dates bookable

## Key Features
- Daily reservations online only
- Hourly rentals walk-up only
- Max 2 shows bookable in advance
- SMS confirmations + reminders
- Admin dashboard for check-in/returns
- Senior (55+) and group (3+) discounts
