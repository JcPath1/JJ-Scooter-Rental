# J & J Scooter Rentals — Shopify Website Project

## Project Overview

Build a Shopify website for J & J Scooter Rentals, a scooter and wagon rental business serving Canton First Monday Trade Days — **the World's Largest Flea Market** — in Canton, Texas. The site needs an integrated booking/scheduling system for customers to reserve rental equipment for specific Trade Days dates, with SMS notifications and an admin dashboard.

**Brand Colors:** Bright Red + Yellow
**Tagline:** "Why Walk When You Can Ride?"
**Marketing Hook:** World's Largest Trade Days — 450+ acres to explore!

## Cancellation Policy

**Cancellation Policy for J & J Scooter Rentals:**

1. **Full Refund:** Cancellations made 72+ hours before the reserved Trade Days date receive a full refund.

2. **50% Refund:** Cancellations made 24-72 hours before the reserved date receive a 50% refund.

3. **No Refund:** Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the reserved date are non-refundable.

4. **No-Shows:** Reservations not picked up within 2 hours of opening time will be released and are non-refundable.

5. **Weather:** In case of event cancellation by First Monday Trade Days due to severe weather, full refunds will be issued.

6. **Modifications:** Reservation date changes are free if made 48+ hours in advance, subject to availability.

7. **Equipment Issues:** If equipment becomes unavailable due to mechanical failure, customers will receive a full refund or substitute equipment.
